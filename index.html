I am an aspiring actor who loves acting and lives by the cinema. Since childhood, I'd never miss a chance to watch any local dramas which used to take place and watching my favourite stars on the big screen always facinated me and have been wishing to see my self on the big screen one day.

My thoughts about acting is that I love how you get to be someone else. It allows one to display all kinds of emotions such as love, worries, problems and become a person who's a fragment of someone's imagination which is written down as a story or a script.

ಪ್ರಮೀಳಾರ್ಜುನೀಯಂ

My character: Vasantha
A William Shakespeare Play Adopted and Written in Kannada by M L Srikantesha Gowda
Directed by Naveen Sanehalli
Performed by ಸಾಫಲ್ಯ ರಂಗ ತಂಡ

Prameelarjuneeyam, a play which unfolds a polygonal love story where Vasantha is in love with Kairave while she has her heart beating only for Jayantha. On the other hand, there is Padmini, who has her liking for Vasantha. Unfortunately, Kairave’s father Menava, admires Vasantha to be his son-in-law. Jayantha and Kairave plan to escape from the chaos caused by Kairave’s exasperated father and the raging Vasantha. Meanwhile, the immortal Phoenix-like, Rathi and Manmatha are having a feud over an abettor boy named Gandharva Bala. The waggery of events occur in the tale when Manmatha’s head mischief-maker Ashuga goes wrong in manouvering Manmatha’s flower trick to help Padmini in her love quest, causing people to fall in love with whoever they see basically after waking up. Apparently, there’s a group of labourers, who are enthralled to prepare a play for Arjuna and Pramiladevi`s wedding where events take a hilarious twist in the story. Can Padmini get Vasantha? Is the play of labourers for Pramilaarjuna’s wedding a success? Did Rathi Devi return the servant boy to Manmatha? The mystical events are fine-tuned with realism, romanticism and laughter.

ವೈಶಂಪಾಯನ ತೀರ

My character: Shambari (System Shambari)
Directed by Savitha (NSD)
Performed by ಸಾಫಲ್ಯ ರಂಗ ತಂಡ

Vaishampayana teera by L N Mukundraj is a play centered around the conflict for supremacy in the artistic life of the two yakshagana performers of Rudragiri. Venkappa hedge & Krishna bhatta. They have their respective fan following venkappa hedge realizes his desire of overpowering his rival in his profession, Krishna Bhatta, by wooing his artist life Kalyani. learning about his wife's submission to venkappa hedge. Krishna bhata disappears from rudragiri in search of a life beyond Kalyan is now the village madwoman. when Krishna bhatta returns after 15yearshis community persuades him to dance in gadha yuddha yakshagana prasanga in which venkappa hedge would be performing Duryodhana & Krishna Bhatta Bhima. The play brings out dual failure of man. Failure in suppressing his animal instinct and failure to climb divinity.

My character: Filch
Performed by ಸಾಫಲ್ಯ ರಂಗ ತಂಡ

Mr. Peachum is the owner of a small organization that makes outfitting and fake props for fake beggars - in a really poor part of the city. He finds out that his daughter, his only child has fallen for and gotten married to the bandit, Macheath. What follows is a crazy ride through a world of thieves, prostitutes, beggars, back alleys, and two timing characters as Mr. and Mrs. Peachum go about trying to imprison Macheath and get back their dear daughter.It is inherently naughty, sexual, playful and with a constant sympathy for the poor and downtrodden.
